Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Anti Vandalism collects message content for moderation purposes. This includes detecting and removing spam, links, and offensive content.

2. Usage of Data

The collected data is used solely for real-time moderation within the Discord server. Anti Vandalism does not store any message content off-platform.

3. What data do we collect?

We collect the following data: - Your Discord User ID - Your Discord User Name - Your Discord User Avatar URL - Your Discord Server ID

4. How do we use this data?

We use this data to provide you with the best possible experience on our services, We also use it to improve our website and services. This includes troubleshooting, data analysis and testing.

5. How do we keep your data safe?

We have implemented security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. We will never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

6. How to access, modify or delete the collected data.

You have the right to access, update or delete your personal data at any time. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us on our official Discord server
